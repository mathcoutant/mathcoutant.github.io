    In Alter Maze you play a little mage who has to escape from a tower in which he's trapped. To do this, you need to use the environment to modify the tower's structure, and thus make your way to the exit.


    Alter Maze is a game made in 48 hours on Unity with a team of 8 people. The game won the UniJAM 2023. I took part in the game design and development. I also did all the game's UI.

    Blob Art is a pixel art game. The player must reproduce pixel art by moving around a grid.
    You can also create your own pixel art and modify existing pixel art.


    This game was created in Java using the LibGDX engine. The team was made up of three people. I was responsible for the graphics and a large part of the code.


    Itch.io GitHub

    Geckolot is a two players game. Each player has a special ability: the axolotl can attract the gecko to itself, while the gecko can repel the axolotl. The two players must help each other to overcome the obstacles of the cave in which they are trapped.


    Geckolot is a game made in 48 hours for the Globale Game JAM 2022 on Unity with a team of 8 people. I was involved in game design, level design and story creation.


    Itch.io GitHub

    Dungeon Survivor


    Dungeon Survivor is a little Hack'n Slash game created for a school project. The goal is to survive as long as possible in an arena by killing monsters and gathering experience to improve your skills.


    It was created with a friend using Unity.
