    Education and experience

    Click on the titles for more information.

    • 2015 - 2019

      I went to the high school "Europe Robert Schuman" in Cholet, France. During my middle and last years in high school I was a member of the CVL (Student Life Council), which enabled me to see how a school was organized.
      It was in high school that I discovered programming with the ISN option (Computer Science and Digital Science). It was also during this period that I first considered making it my profession. During my final year of high school, I created my first video game in Processing for my final ISN project.
      I obtained my scientific baccalaureate with the congratulations of the jury (18.5/20 average).

    • Summer 2019

      During the summer of 2019, I volunteered at the SNEJ (National and European Youth Week), a week reserved for minors and dedicated to discovering a region by bike. The 2019 edition was held in La Flèche, in France.
      Having already participated several times as a young person, I decided to volunteer. So during the week I got to see the organizational side of the event. I was in charge of orienting the youngsters, preparing the facilities and I also assessed the participants in the national criterium which takes place every year during the week.

    • 2019 - 2021

      After high school, I was accepted in preparatory class at the Lycée Clémenceau in Nantes. It consists of two years of intensive study in mathematics and physics.
      During this period one of my favorite courses was Python. That's why I decided to continue studying IT at Télécom Sudparis.

    • 2021 - 2024

      It was at Télécom Sudparis that I developed my programming skills the most. You can find a detailed list of all the projects I have carried out over the past 3 years on the Project page.
      In my second year, I was vice-president of the game creation club (video games, board games, escape games, etc.), which was the club that stimulated me the most during this period.

    • Summer 2022

      During this nine-week internship I discovered Ruby on Rails and the model-view-controller software architecture. Concretely, I coded several APIs:
      - The first one receives address search requests and redirects them to the corresponding service (BAN or photon). It allowed me to get to grips with Ruby on Rails.
      - A second that receives SMS requests, forwards them to the SendInBlue service, and tracks the status of the SMS to send informative webhooks back to the customer.

    • Summer 2023

      For 4 weeks I attended the "Business Data Science with Python" course offered by Berlin's Technische Universität.
      The course epxlores several data analysis methods such as regression, classification and clustering.. It enabled me to deepen my knowledge of Data Science and familiarize myself with dedicated Python libraries (Numpy, Pandas, Scipy).
